Greek Letter Symbols in Geospatial Calculations
φ (Phi) Symbol

    Represents Latitude
    Indicates the angular distance north or south of the equator
    Ranges from -90° to 90°
    Negative values: Southern Hemisphere
    Positive values: Northern Hemisphere
    0°: Equator

λ (Lambda) Symbol

    Represents Longitude
    Indicates the angular distance east or west of the prime meridian
    Ranges from -180° to 180°
    Negative values: Western Hemisphere
    Positive values: Eastern Hemisphere
    0°: Prime Meridian (Greenwich, UK)
